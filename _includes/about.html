About Us

Empowering Students through Free Non-Profit Tutoring

  • 2020

    Our Educational Initiative Begins

    Dedicated to the academic success of students, we started our journey in 2020 with a vision to provide free non-profit tutoring services for elementary, middle, and high school students (K-12).

  • Biweekly, Monday & Friday

    Guided Learning Sessions

    Join us every Monday and Friday from 4:30 to 5:30 PM PST for engaging biweekly tutoring sessions covering Math, English, and Science. Our qualified tutors are committed to providing personalized, one-on-one assistance to help students thrive academically.

  • Session Monitoring

    Parental Involvement

    To keep parents informed about their child's progress, our sessions are diligently monitored by administrators. We provide regular updates to parents, ensuring transparency and collaboration in the educational journey.

  • Individualized Support

    Empowering Every Student

    Our 1-on-1 sessions are tailored to each student's needs, focusing on completing assignments, understanding concepts, and preparing for upcoming tests and quizzes. We are committed to helping every student achieve their best!
